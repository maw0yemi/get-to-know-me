LSC103 Principles of Biomedical Science

Description

This module introduces the fundamental principles of scientific discovery in the context of medicine.

Aims

The aim of this Module is to provide the student with an understanding of scientific methodology in the context of medicine and bioscience with an emphasis on the science of clinical trials and epidemiology.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module I was be able to :

  1. Demonstrate an understanding of scientific research principles in the context of medicine and bioscience.
  2. Demonstrate an understanding of concepts of clinical studies and epidemiology in the context of medicine and bioscience
