National Higher Certificate

FR2435 Networking Technology: In this module, I learned the fundamentals of computer networking, including network architecture, protocols, and configurations. I gained hands-on experience in setting up and managing networks.

J0H934 Data Security: This module taught me how to protect sensitive data by exploring encryption techniques, secure storage methods, and effective data management practices to prevent breaches.

J0HA34 Computer Programming: I developed my programming skills with a focus on cybersecurity applications. Using scripting languages, I automated tasks, analysed data, and created simple security tools.

J0HB34 Penetration: In this module, I was introduced to ethical hacking techniques to identify vulnerabilities in systems and networks. I conducted penetration tests and presented my findings professionally.

J0HC34 Internet of Things: I explored the security challenges of the Internet of Things, studying IoT devices, architecture, and protocols while learning how to secure interconnected systems.

J0HE34 Securing Network Devices: This module focused on securing essential network hardware like routers and switches. I learned how to configure and protect these devices from vulnerabilities and threats.

J0HH34 Professionalism and Ethics in Cyber Security: I developed an understanding of the ethical and professional responsibilities within the field of cybersecurity, including legal considerations, ethical decision-making, and industry standards.

J0HJ34 Computer Networking: Concepts, Practice and Introduction to Security: In this module, I gained a broad understanding of networking concepts with a strong emphasis on security. I learned to identify and mitigate common networking threats.

J0HK34 Ethical Hacking: I deepened my knowledge of ethical hacking by studying advanced methodologies and tools. This module allowed me to practise simulating real-world scenarios in a safe environment.

J0HL34 Digital Forensics: This module taught me how to investigate cybercrimes by analysing digital evidence. I learned to use forensic tools, apply investigative techniques, and understand the legal aspects of evidence handling.

J0J734 Cyber Security: Graded Unit 1: Through this project-based module, I integrated the knowledge and skills I gained across the course. I worked on a practical cybersecurity challenge, demonstrating my abilities and understanding.

J0J8 34 Computer Architecture: I explored the components that make up computer systems, both hardware and software. This module helped me understand how systems are designed, built, and optimised for performance.

J2JW34 Firewall Essentials: This module focused on the importance of firewalls in protecting networks. I learned to configure, monitor, and maintain firewalls to defend against external threats.
