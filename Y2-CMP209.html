CMP209-Digital Forensics

Description

This module offers a comprehensive exploration into the techniques and methodologies used to investigate and analyze digital evidence. Students will delve into the intricate processes of uncovering, preserving, and interpreting digital data crucial for legal and investigative purposes

Aims

To equip students with the foundational knowledge and practical skills necessary to investigate, analyze, and interpret digital evidence, ensuring adherence to ethical, legal, and professional standards in digital forensics.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module I was able to:

  1. Demonstrate the ability to identify, extract, and analyze digital artifacts from various devices and storage media using industry-standard tools and techniques.
  2. Apply appropriate forensic procedures to preserve the integrity of digital evidence while maintaining compliance with legal and ethical considerations.
  3. Develop clear and concise forensic reports, effectively communicating findings to technical and non-technical audiences, including in legal or investigative contexts.
