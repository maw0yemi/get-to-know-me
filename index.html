Who am I?

I am a Spanish-Nigerian Ethical Hacking student at Abertay University, Scotland. In 2017, during my high school years, the worldwide WannaCry attack happened. Seeing the cyberspace crumbling was the pivotal event that sparked my interest in cybersecurity. From there, I started learning the basics of micro-computing systems and networking. Once the basics were established, I focused on the security aspect, learning the importance of security and how to implement it. As I kept educating myself, I was introduced to Ethical Hacking.

I have learned how to summarize it in one sentence: I can build, secure, and perform a penetration test on computer systems, networks, and web applications. Feel free to explore my website to learn more about me, my background, and my aspirations!
