Student Event Helper Experience

Job description

The small and friendly Events Team recruited me as a Student Event Helper to assist with the busy and exciting schedule of events planned throughout the year. Offer Holder Days, Open Days, the Abertay Digital Graduate Show, Graduation, and conferences were just a few of the events that I covered! There are various aspects to delivering a successful event - from setup to end result - and my input was valuable. When delivering events on campus, I was involved in a range of activities – from helping with signage around the University, preparing goody bags, badges, registration desks, welcoming and directing guests, answering queries, or finding the right person to answer the query, in general being on hand and ready to help. Student Event Helpers also assisted with delivering a wide range of online events.

Events Worked In: Open days, Offer holder days, EYEE conference, Graduation, Abertay Digital Graduate Show, CyberQuarter Events, High School visits, Campus tours, Freshers week, Prizegiving, Student term registration, International welcome & workshops, BBC Dundee radio festival.

Skills Gained:
  • Communication: Effectively conveyed information and answered queries from attendees.
  • Planning: Assisted in the organization and execution of various events, ensuring all logistics were in place.
  • Customer Service: Provided support to guests, ensuring a positive event experience.
  • Problem Solving: Quickly addressed and resolved any issues that arose during events.
  • Teamwork: Collaborated with other student helpers and event staff to achieve common goals.
  • Time Management: Managed multiple tasks and responsibilities efficiently during events.
  • Attention to Detail: Ensured all aspects of event preparation and execution were accurate.
  • Adaptability: Adjusted to last-minute changes and unexpected challenges.
  • Networking: Built connections with university staff, event organizers, and attendees.
  • Public Speaking: Communicated announcements and instructions clearly to large groups.
  • Marketing and Promotion: Assisted with the promotion of events through social media and other channels.
  • Interpersonal Skills: Interacted with a diverse range of people and built strong professional relationships.
